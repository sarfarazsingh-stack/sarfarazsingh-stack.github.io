About Coffee Connects

Coffee Connects believes that some of the best ideas happen during casual conversations.
What's Brewing is our lightweight platform dedicated to creating welcoming spaces for local and virtual coffee sessions. Here, you can discover curated conversations, connect by interests, and turn shared moments into new opportunities together.

Discover

Find curated coffee conversations by topic, time zone, and host expertise.

Host

Create sessions, set capacity, and add prompts to guide thoughtful conversations.

Connect

Meet like-minded attendees and follow up after the session for lasting connection.

Community

Explore our social channels to stay in touch with Coffee Connects. Find co-hosts for your sessions and share ideas with others who believe in coffee-powered connections.
